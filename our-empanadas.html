We believe shared flavor is the best flavor

Lunfardo Empanadas is the true taste of Argentine style gourmet cuisine, dedicated to the craft of making artisan empanadas, unique in Charleston Metro Area, South Carolina.

Cooking since 2018, when the three founders decided they wanted to bring a piece of their lovely birth country to South Carolina and Florida. It started small, very small, but a tasty good product and warm wholesome made our dream grow.

In 2022 we opened our first permanent location in Charleston Metro Area, SC and we’ve been lucky enough to keep growing since then.

Hello there! At the moment we are only offering our frozen empanadas. Very soon you will enjoy our baked empanadas, ready to take to your table and share with friends!
